About Me

Hi there! My name is Holly Abrams and I have a passion for web development. It's like solving a puzzle in which the result is something useful (and cool) that can be shared with others.

My journey into software engineering has been a long one. I spent a few years in construction project accounting, but kept looking for ways to tap into my creative talents.

That's when it happened; I was provided an opportunity to build an internal knowledge base and was hooked on the limitless possibilities a little bit of code can provide for a website. Web development fulfills my passion for creativity and problem-solving.

I'm a huge Disney fan, so in my free time, I like to travel to Walt Disney World and Universal Studios, Florida.
